About Semeion

Semeion is a watching eye that notices what others miss. Brands, films, books, and the small formal choices inside them, the name, the colour, the cut, that quietly tell someone who to be.

I'm interested in that mechanism the way a linguist is interested in grammar, not because the sentence is beautiful, but because it's built.

We don't follow the noise. We decode what drives it.
